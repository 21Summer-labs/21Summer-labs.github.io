FAQs

Why not just hire a full-time developer?

Full-time senior full-stack developers typically cost north of $120,000, plus benefits– not to mention, available ones are hard to find. Plus, you might not always have work for them.

Our monthly subscription means you have the flexibility to only pay for when you need them, and you can resume when you have excess engineering work again.

Do you work on our Github repository?

We can either create a repository for your project, fork an existing one, or work on your repository once you've given us access.

You can indicate where we should add our code during your onboarding process, once you purchase a subscription.

How long will each task/request take?

Most requests take 2-4 business days to fulfil. More complex requests may take longer, while simpler requests may take less time.

If a request is too complex/large, it may be broken down into sub-requests, which will be worked on individually. We'll let you know whenever that happens.

What tools are used to communicate?

The main tools we use are Slack and Linear. Slack will be used for communication with our founder, who can answer any questions you have about what's going on, or how things work. Linear will be used for all the task management.

What if I have a very large task?

If you give us a large and complex task, we may sometimes break it down into smaller sub-tasks. We'll let you know when this happens.

This can help you keep track of our progress, and the timeline until completion.

How does the 24/7 support work?

You have direct access to our work coordinators & founder via Slack, who are also developers, and can help answer any questions you might have about the work that is going on, or anything else. If you need, you can also schedule a call with them.

How does pausing work?

If you don't have enough work to fill an entire month, you can pause once your tasks are completed. However many days are left in your billing cycle can be used once you resume the subscription.

Billing cycles are 31 days. If you pause on day 20, you will have 11 days of service remaining when you resume.

How do I make new requests?

Create a new issue on Linear, and attach any relevant links or documents. Loom videos, Figma links, Google docs– all fair game.

We'll let you know if we have any questions or concerns through Slack.

What if I don't like the work?

Not a problem! We'll continue to revise the work until you're completely satisfied.

What if I just need one task done?

That's absolutely fine. You can pause your subscription once the work is completed, and save the remainder of your subscription for when you next have some more work.

How does the satisfaction guarantee work?

If within the first month, you're not happy with our service and work, you can let us know over Slack you'd like to issue a refund, and we'll close your account, and return your money in full.

Please note this only applies to the first billing cycle.
